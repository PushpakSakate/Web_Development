Maharashtra this one word says it all. From Bollywood to Dharavi, from Mumbai to Pune, from Ganpatipule to Marine drive, from Badlapur to Bandra and from Siddhivinayak to Mt. Mary. Popularly known for its dream city Mumbai, Maharashtra is probably the most diverse as well as balanced states in India.
Maharashtra is home to a number of beaches that are definitely worth a visit. Kashid, Ganpatiplue, Alibaug, Tarkarli, Dapoli and Harihareshwar are a few beaches that simply add up to the pristine beauty of this state.
The natives of the state are known as Maharashtrians and despite the large variety of food available here, the local dishes and cuisine is utterly delicious.
